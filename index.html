Outreach Opportunity Notes

Why These Are Strong Leads

Each business in this list demonstrates a clear combination of active digital marketing spend (Meta ads / Instagram) and a missing or inadequate web presence. This gap represents a direct, quantifiable pain point that a web designer can solve.

Businesses running paid social ads without a website are essentially paying to send traffic to Instagram — a platform they don't own and can't fully control. A professional website would give them a conversion-optimised destination, booking functionality, SEO visibility, and brand credibility.
